Why choose FixTrax

There is no doubt FixTrax is one of the best designed software to manage your mechanic shop because its designed ground up by mechanics like you. And to make it even better, what drives future development is also mechanics like you who give us regular feedback and request features. Here are just a few features to get you started.

USER MANAGEMENT

With FixTrax, you can manage each employee and their user accounts through our user management section. You can fully customize users and their permissions with multi-level user permissioning system.

VEHICLE MANAGEMENT

You can manage all of your customer vehicles very efficiently and effortlessly. Adding a new vehicle is a breeze with our vehicle selector. You can also automatically add vehicles using their VIN numbers.

CUSTOMER MANAGEMENT

Our application will let you manage customers, view their past orders, and manage all of the vehicles owned by that customer. As always adding new customers is as easy as 1, 2, 3.

ORDER MANAGEMENT

You can manage all aspects of your customer's order from our order management screen. From parts, labor, and payment history to order status and customer notifications. All in one screen.

LOCATION MANAGEMENT

Have more than one location? no problem, you can manage them all with a single login. You can even add location specific users so they can only access data for that one location they belong to.

CUSTOMER NOTIFICATIONS

Why worry about notifying your customers or spend hours contacting customers? Our new and improved customer notifications will automatically send text/email alerts and also make automated phone calls for you to inform the customer.
